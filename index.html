About Us

Nutroforge products was founded in 2021, with the main goal to produce best quality food products that are nutritious as well as environmentally friendly as our RnD teams works on the moto that “WASTE IS WAELTH”. The mind behind the vision is of Yusra Kokab who is the CEO of the company. Our vision to produce highly nutritious food with low costing is inspired by the increasing number of malnutrition cases in Pakistan. With the growth of the company we have also decided to share a percentage of our profit to different organization and NGOS and to support several ration drives.
